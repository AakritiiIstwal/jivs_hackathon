This table stores information about registered users on the platform, capturing personal details, contact information, and account-specific data. The key columns include a unique identifier for each user, the user's first name, last name, email address, and phone number. The table also includes the user's date of birth, home address, city of residence, state, postal code, and the date the user registered their account. Additionally, the table contains columns for account status (such as active or inactive), and the last login date.
